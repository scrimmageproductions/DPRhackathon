Tracks

Art

In the Art Track, participants are encouraged to unleash their creativity and showcase innovative ideas in the realm of digital art. This track focuses on the use of cutting-edge technology to enhance the creation, ownership, and distribution of digital art, as well as exploring new forms of artistic expression that are native to the digital world.

Systems/Operations

In the Systems/Operations Track, the focus is on optimizing and streamlining the processes and systems that make up the backbone of businesses and organizations. Participants in this track will develop innovative solutions using advanced technology to improve efficiency, security, and transparency in areas such as supply chain management, identity verification, and data storage.

Ordering/Delivery

In the Ordering/Delivery Track, the focus is on revolutionizing the way we order and receive goods and services. This track encourages the use of cutting-edge technology to create new platforms and systems for ordering, tracking, and delivering products and services, with a focus on improving customer experience, reducing costs, and increasing efficiency.
